Schindler's List

Genre: Biography, History
Released Year: 1993
Main Hero: Liam Neeson
Short description: In German-occupied Poland during World War II, Oskar Schindler gradually becomes concerned for his Jewish workforce after witnessing their persecution by the Nazi Germans.

Genre: Action, Crime

Short description: A martial artist agrees to spy on a reclusive crime lord using his invitation to a tournament there as cover.
